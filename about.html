I am proficient in React, Redux, JavaScript, HTML and CSS. On the backend, I have worked with Node.js, Express, and PostgreSQL in addition to less familiar technologies such as hapi and knex. I have also worked extensively several third party packages such as Retool, Expo, and React Native.
